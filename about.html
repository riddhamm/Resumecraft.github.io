About CampusCV

CampusCV is a simple resume builder designed for students and recent bachelor graduates.

Our goal is to provide clean, recruiter‑friendly templates and guided tips so first‑time job seekers can create professional resumes quickly and confidently. [web:7][web:8]

  • Templates optimized for internships, entry‑level jobs and campus placements.
  • Real‑time preview so users can see how their resume looks while typing.
  • Easy export to print or PDF using the browser print dialog.
