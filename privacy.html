Privacy policy

CampusCV respects your privacy and handles your resume data responsibly.

This example policy is for demonstration only; you must review it with a legal professional before using it on a live site.

Information we collect

When you use the resume builder, you may enter personal information such as your name, contact details, education, skills and work history. This information is used only to display and generate your resume for you.

How we use information

Your resume content is processed to create previews and downloadable versions of your resume. We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising.

Data storage

You can choose whether your resume is stored on our servers or kept only in your browser. If storage is enabled in the future, we will clearly explain how long we keep your data and how you can delete it.

Children and students

This service is intended for users who are students or graduates aged 16 or older. If you are under the minimum age in your country, you should use the service only with parental or guardian consent. [web:9]

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at privacy@campuscv.example.
